The Metropolitan Building is located in Long Island City, Queens. You can stay there, or in Brooklyn or Manhattan, in which case the E, M, 7, and G trains allow for easy access.

We have a few hotel recommendations for each area on this page. Book now, or if you're feeling lucky, shoot for a last minute deal the week before. And don't forget about the AirBnB option - our preferred way to travel.

Feel free to ask us directly for additional neighborhood and hotel advice.

Brooklyn

AirBnB search

Nu Hotel

The NU Hotel is a 93 room boutique in Downtown Brooklyn, on Smith St just north of Atlantic Ave. It's a few blocks from the Hoyt-Schermerhorn stop (A, C, and G trains).

Hotel Le Bleu

Hotel Le Bleu is a 48 room hotel on 4th Ave in Park Slope. It's a pretty short walk from the 4 Ave F/G stop, and the 9th St stop on the R train. We live a few blocks away.

Gershwin Hotel

Located on 27th St between Fifth Ave and Madison, convenient to the F, M, N, R, 6, and 1 trains.

Z Hotel New York

A 3 minute walk from the Metropolitan Building on 43rd Ave in Long Island City, this is a new-ish 100 room boutique hotel. We don't have first-hand knowledge, but from the reviews on TripAdvisor, it sounds like a good choice. There is a room block here as well, with 9 Queen rooms and 1 King room reserved through May 3, for $180 and $205/night, respectively.
